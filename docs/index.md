This site hosts my research note on the standard and generalized parameter-shift rules for variational quantum circuits.

[Read the full paper](./parameter_shift_proof_paper.pdf)

This note presents a prose-based exposition of the parameter-shift method for differentiating quantum expectation values. 
It includes a worked single-qubit example, the generalized theorem for multi-eigenvalue generators and a discussion of the geometric intuition behind quantum differentiation.
